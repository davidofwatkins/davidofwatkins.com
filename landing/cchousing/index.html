Champlain College Housing Registration

Web Application (Senior Capstone Project, Proof-of-Concept)

As a Champlain College capstone project, this housing registration web application is a working proof-of-concept for what would allow students to research on-campus living opportunities with details regarding location, room size, roommate possibilities, and more. Likewise, it would allow residential administrators to enter descriptions and upload images and floor plans for each hall using an intuitive and centralized control panel.

Save time: register anywhere, anytime

Champlain College's current registration process involves physical priority numbers, scheduled registration meetings, long lines, and long wait times. This web app replaces this slow, inefficient system with a flexible and fast online alternative. Designed to work like online class registration, students can log in using their normal accounts during their specified window of time from anywhere in the world, whether they be at home, studying abroad, or on campus.

Make better choices

It's not always easy to pick a residence hall without much information about it. This solution provides an overview of details for a given hall on a single page, allowing students to make more educated decisions at a glance about where they choose to live. This could be especially important for incoming freshmen or transfer students who are not familiar with campus.

Collaborate with roommates

It's easy to choose a roommate. Students with earlier registration windows can specify roommates when finalizing their registration and post a short bio about themselves for potential roommates who might register later.

Administration Benefits: faster, cheaper, more efficient

The Department of Residential Life benefits, too. Administrators can upload pictures and floor plans, link halls with Foursquare locations, override housing assignments, and more all from a centralized control panel - no need to manage events or mail-in registration.

Hall Overview
All on One Page

Students who log into the registration site are greeted with a map and list of all residence halls. After choosing a hall, they will see a page with detailed information about it, including a description, slideshow of images, floor plans, map, and foursquare tips.

Intuitive Rooom Selection

Looking for a room has never been easier. Students can browse floor plans and quickly identify available rooms.

Administration:
Centralized Control Panel

Housing managers can add, remove, and modify residence hall information or manually register other students easily and instantly from an administrative dashboard.

Administration:
Upload Floorplans & Draw

The administration panel makes it easy for housing management to upload floor plans, add room overlays, and enter information about each room including size, maximum number of residence, gender restrictions, and more.

Disclaimer: this housing registration system was created as a proof-of-concept and a senior web development capstone project at Champlain College. While much of it is functional, not every feature has been fully polished and tested.
